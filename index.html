Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and delivery options.

How do I join the Wellness Program?
You can join our Wellness Program by signing up on our website. Choose your plan, and we’ll deliver customized herbal blends monthly.
What’s the difference between a tincture and a tea?
Tinctures are concentrated herbal extracts taken in small doses, while teas are gentler infusions of herbs steeped in water.
Do I need a subscription to purchase products?
No, you can purchase herbal products individually. However, our Wellness Program offers exclusive benefits and discounts.
Can I change my delivery preferences if needed?
Yes, you can modify your delivery preferences at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.
Is my order information kept secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to ensure your order details and personal information are secure.
How long does shipping usually take?
Shipping typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location and the shipping option you choose.
What is your return policy?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our support within 7 days for a full refund or replacement.
Can I gift a Wellness Program subscription?
Yes! You can purchase gift subscriptions and have them delivered directly to the recipient’s address.
Do you offer consultations or events?
We offer personalized consultations and wellness events. Check our newsletter and events page for updates.
